Notice Regarding Membership

Only those who complete membership forms will be included on the shul's membership list

the shtiebel does not have annual dinners, a journal, or appeals during the year to raise money, and we don't send out bills for dues. We rely only on your membership contributions to pay the cost of running the shul

last year, one-third of those membership list still have not paid their dues

when you deliver a check to the shul, please specify if it is for membership dues, the kol nidre appeal, an aliya, kiddish, shalosh seudos or other purpose

to maintain the dignity of our shabbos services, please sponsor at least one kiddish and shalosh seudos each year ‐ you don't need a special occasion

all regular members who complete a membership form but cannot afford to pay membership dues will still be included in the membership list
non-regular members who do not pay dues will not be included

everyone is welcome to daven in the shtiebel whether or not they can afford to pay membership dues
